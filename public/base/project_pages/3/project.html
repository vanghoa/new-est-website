Tabao Tammy superhero

For the second half of level 2 Design Communication at LASALLE College of the Arts, we are asked to create a visual identity for a fictional character, a superhero with superhuman abilities. The outcome must involve not only the usual identity assets like logos or typeface system, but also an illustrated character design in action printed on an A3 poster.

I also crafted a custom display typeface that matches the feeling of the logotype. However, I don't have much time; therefore, the glyph list is sort of limited, only the Latin letters and numerical characters. The typeface is characterized by the short x-height in relation to the body width, mimicking the computer graphics look back then. The typeface also introduces a blackslanted structure that would make it even more like an extra-extended font.
